# AI MARKETING LEARNING PLAN — Paula Hernandez
**Goal:** Become fluent in AI-powered marketing and paid ads in 90 days
**Daily commitment:** 2–3 hours | Start date: February 2026

---

## PHASE 1 — WEEKS 1–3: FOUNDATIONS
*Goal: Understand how AI is reshaping paid ads and marketing*

### Daily Schedule (2.5 hrs)
| Block | Time | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Block 1 | 45 min | Course / structured learning |
| Block 2 | 45 min | Hands-on practice / tool exploration |
| Block 3 | 30 min | Read 1 article or watch 1 short video |
| Block 4 | 30 min | Apply to your own profile or mock campaign |

### Week 1 — AI Marketing Fundamentals
- [ ] **Day 1–2:** What is AI in marketing? Watch Google's "AI in Marketing" overview (YouTube)
- [ ] **Day 3–4:** Meta Advantage+ explained — how Facebook's AI auto-optimizes ads
- [ ] **Day 5–6:** Google Performance Max — how Google's AI campaign type works
- [ ] **Day 7:** Review + write down your 3 biggest takeaways

**Free resources:**
- Meta Blueprint: business.facebook.com/learn (free courses)
- Google Skillshop: skillshop.google.com (free, leads to certification)

### Week 2 — Paid Ads Basics (Meta & Google)
- [ ] **Day 1–2:** Meta Ads Manager — campaign structure (Campaign > Ad Set > Ad)
- [ ] **Day 3–4:** Audience targeting: Custom Audiences, Lookalikes, Interest targeting
- [ ] **Day 5–6:** Google Ads — Search vs Display vs Performance Max
- [ ] **Day 7:** Set up a FREE Meta Ads Manager account, explore the interface

**Action:** Create a mock campaign for a fake brand — just set it up, don't spend money

### Week 3 — AI Content Tools for Marketing
- [ ] **Day 1:** Claude for marketing — write ad copy, headlines, A/B test variations
- [ ] **Day 2:** ChatGPT for marketing briefs, audience personas, campaign concepts
- [ ] **Day 3:** Canva AI — Magic Write, AI image generation for ads
- [ ] **Day 4:** Jasper or Copy.ai — AI copywriting tools used by marketing teams
- [ ] **Day 5–6:** Practice: Use Claude to write 5 Meta ad variations for a brand you know
- [ ] **Day 7:** Build a mini "AI Tools Stack" document for your resume/portfolio

---

## PHASE 2 — WEEKS 4–6: INTERMEDIATE
*Goal: Run and analyze real (or simulated) campaigns. Start certifications.*

### Week 4 — Meta Ads Deep Dive
- [ ] Meta Blueprint: "Introduction to Facebook Advertising" (free course)
- [ ] Learn: Campaign objectives (Awareness vs Traffic vs Conversions vs Leads)
- [ ] Learn: Pixel setup, retargeting, event tracking
- [ ] Practice: Build a full mock Meta campaign targeting Hispanic audiences in the U.S.
- [ ] Study: How Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns and AI bidding work

### Week 5 — Google Ads + Analytics
- [ ] Google Skillshop: Google Ads Search Certification (free, ~6 hrs)
- [ ] Learn: Smart Bidding (Target CPA, Target ROAS, Maximize Conversions)
- [ ] Learn: Google Analytics 4 basics — reading campaign performance data
- [ ] Practice: Build a mock Google Search campaign for a FinTech or multicultural brand
- [ ] Connect: How AI bidding + audience signals = Performance Max

### Week 6 — AI Strategy + Prompt Engineering for Marketing
- [ ] Learn: How to write effective prompts for marketing tasks
- [ ] Practice daily with Claude:
  - Campaign briefs
  - Audience targeting strategy
  - Ad copy A/B tests
  - Competitor analysis
  - Monthly marketing plans
- [ ] Build: A "Prompt Library" — save your best prompts for future jobs

**Milestone:** By end of Week 6 — complete Google Ads Search Certification

---

## PHASE 3 — WEEKS 7–12: ADVANCED + PORTFOLIO
*Goal: Get certified. Build real portfolio. Apply for jobs confidently.*

### Week 7–8 — Meta Certification
- [ ] Complete Meta Blueprint: "Meta Certified Digital Marketing Associate" (paid, ~$99)
- [ ] Study: AI features in Meta — Advantage+, automated placements, dynamic creative
- [ ] Practice: Create a case study using your MyBambu influencer/social results + paid strategy

### Week 9–10 — Analytics & Reporting
- [ ] Learn: How to read ROAS, CPM, CPC, CTR, Conversion Rate
- [ ] Build: A sample marketing dashboard (use Google Looker Studio — free)
- [ ] Practice: Take your MyBambu engagement numbers and build a performance narrative

### Week 11–12 — Portfolio Building
- [ ] Build 3 portfolio pieces:
  1. A mock paid social campaign (Meta) for a multicultural brand
  2. An AI-generated content strategy doc (using Claude/ChatGPT)
  3. A campaign performance analysis with real or simulated data
- [ ] Update your LinkedIn with new skills + certifications
- [ ] Add "AI Marketing" and "Paid Media" to your resume skills section
